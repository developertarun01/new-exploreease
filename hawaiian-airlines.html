      About Hawaiian Airlines

      Hawaiian Airlines, founded in 1929, is the largest airline serving the State of Hawaii and one of the longest-operating carriers in the United States. Headquartered in Honolulu, Hawaii, the airline connects the Hawaiian Islands with major cities across the U.S. mainland as well as select international destinations in Asia and the South Pacific. Operating primarily from Daniel K. Inouye International Airport in Honolulu, Hawaiian Airlines provides both interisland and long-haul services known for Hawaiian-inspired hospitality and onboard comfort.       Hawaiian Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers flying with Hawaiian Airlines may complete check-in either at airport counters, self-service kiosks, or through online check-in options. Online check-in is commonly preferred because it saves time and allows travelers to select seats before arriving at the airport. Some travel situations may require airport verification or document checks. For assistance with check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete procedures smoothly. For most domestic flights, check-in generally closes about 45 minutes before departure, while international flights may require earlier completion depending on airport requirements.
      2. Online Check-in: Hawaiian Airlines online check-in typically opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until approximately 60 minutes prior to scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Hawaiian Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      HawaiianMiles is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers with mileage earning opportunities. Members earn miles through Hawaiian Airlines flights, partner airlines, hotel bookings, car rentals, and affiliated financial partners.
      Miles can be redeemed for award flights, seat upgrades, or other travel-related benefits.
      The program includes membership tiers such as Pualani Gold and Pualani Platinum, offering benefits like priority services and additional travel privileges.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Hawaiian Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowances vary depending on route type, fare category, and cabin class. Fees and inclusions may differ between interisland, mainland U.S., and international travel.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item at no additional cost. Carry-on baggage dimensions generally should not exceed 22 inches x 14 inches x 9 inches, including handles and wheels.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage may be included on certain international or premium fares, while standard economy fares may require additional fees. Checked bags typically allow up to 50 lbs (23 kg) per bag, with higher allowances available for premium cabins or elite members.
      Special baggage such as surfboards, sports equipment, or musical instruments may be accepted according to airline guidelines, reflecting the airline’s focus on leisure travel to Hawaii.
      Passengers can add checked baggage while booking Hawaiian Airlines flight tickets through our platform.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Hawaiian Airlines Flight Changes

      Hawaiian Airlines allows itinerary modifications depending on fare type, route, and ticket conditions selected during booking. Many fares permit passengers to change travel dates or flight timings through the manage booking feature, while certain discounted fares may include restrictions. Same-day flight changes may be available on eligible routes subject to seat availability. When modifying a booking, travelers may need to pay any applicable fare difference if the new flight costs more. Reviewing fare rules before requesting changes helps passengers manage travel plans smoothly and avoid unexpected charges.

      Hawaiian Airlines Flight Cancellation

      Hawaiian Airlines provides cancellation options designed to offer flexibility when travel plans change. Eligible tickets may be canceled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund if booking conditions are met. Refundable fares qualify for monetary refunds, while non-refundable tickets are typically converted into travel credits for future use. If the airline cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers may request refunds or alternative rebooking arrangements. Travel credits issued after voluntary cancellations usually remain valid for a limited period, allowing customers to reschedule trips later. Policies may vary by route or fare category, making it important to review ticket terms before canceling.

      International Flights & Routes

      Hawaiian Airlines International Flights

      Hawaiian Airlines operates international flights connecting Hawaii with destinations across Asia, Oceania, and select Pacific regions. The airline also provides extensive service between Hawaii and major cities on the U.S. mainland. Multiple cabin options, including Economy and Premium Cabin experiences, are available on long-haul routes. International flights commonly include complimentary meals, entertainment systems, and generous baggage allowances designed to enhance passenger comfort during extended journeys. Honolulu serves as a central gateway, enabling smooth connections between islands and international destinations.

      Hawaiian Airlines International Routes

      Hawaiian Airlines maintains a route network linking Honolulu and other Hawaiian islands with cities such as Los Angeles, San Francisco, Seattle, Tokyo, Seoul, Sydney, and Auckland. Routes are adjusted periodically based on travel demand and tourism patterns. The airline’s scheduling supports both leisure travelers visiting Hawaii and residents traveling abroad. Through airline partnerships, passengers can continue onward journeys beyond Hawaii using coordinated connections, creating a seamless travel experience with integrated baggage handling and simplified itineraries.
