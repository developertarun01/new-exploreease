      About Etihad Airways

      Etihad Airways, established in 2003, is the national airline of the United Arab Emirates and operates from its headquarters in Abu Dhabi. The airline connects passengers to more than 70 destinations across the Middle East, Europe, Asia, Africa, Australia, and North America through its primary hub at Zayed International Airport, Abu Dhabi. Known for its premium travel experience and modern fleet, Etihad focuses mainly on international long-haul and medium-haul services.       Etihad Airways Check-in Methods

      Passengers traveling with Etihad Airways may complete check-in either at airport counters or through digital check-in options. Online check-in is commonly preferred as it reduces waiting time and allows advance seat selection. Certain travel documents or special assistance cases may require airport check-in verification. For any check-in or boarding support, travelers may contact Exploreease customer assistance.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers are advised to arrive early to complete procedures smoothly. Check-in for most international flights generally closes 60 minutes before departure, though timing may vary depending on airport regulations. Passengers should confirm deadlines before travel.

      Etihad Airways Frequent Flyer Program

      Etihad Guest is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers through mileage earning and redemption opportunities. Members earn miles when flying with Etihad or partner airlines, as well as through hotel stays, car rentals, shopping, and financial partners connected with the program.
      Earned miles can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, or travel-related rewards.
      The program offers multiple membership tiers including Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum, providing increasing benefits such as priority services and additional baggage allowances.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Etihad Airways Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowances depend on fare type, travel route, and cabin class. Fees and limits may vary based on ticket category, loyalty tier, and destination.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are generally allowed one cabin bag and one personal item without additional charge. Standard cabin baggage dimensions should not exceed approximately 56 cm x 36 cm x 23 cm, with weight limits varying according to travel class.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is usually included under Etihad’s weight-based allowance system. Economy Class passengers typically receive allowances between 20 kg and 35 kg, while Business and First Class travelers may carry higher limits depending on fare conditions.
      Additional baggage can be purchased online before departure at discounted rates.
      Special items such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be accepted subject to airline guidelines.
      Passengers can add checked baggage while booking Etihad Airways flight tickets through our platform.
