      About ITA Airways

      ITA Airways, established in 2020, is Italy’s national airline and the successor to Alitalia. Headquartered in Rome, the airline operates from its main hub at Leonardo da Vinci International Airport (Rome Fiumicino), connecting Italy with major destinations across Europe, North America, South America, the Middle East, and Asia. ITA Airways focuses on providing modern full-service travel with an emphasis on Italian hospitality, efficiency, and comfort. The airline maintains codeshare and alliance partnerships that extend connectivity to numerous global destinations beyond its own network.

      The program includes membership tiers such as Smart, Plus, Premium, and Executive, offering increasing privileges including priority services and enhanced travel comfort.

      ITA Airways operates international flights connecting Italy with major cities across Europe, North America, South America, and parts of Asia and the Middle East.
