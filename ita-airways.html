      About ITA Airways

      ITA Airways, established in 2020, is the national airline of Italy, with headquarters located in Rome. The carrier connects passengers to key domestic and international destinations across Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, and Asia. Its primary hub is Rome Fiumicino Airport, supported by Milan Linate Airport for regional operations. Exploreease allows travelers to make ITA Airways flight reservations easily through a convenient online platform. We also feature selected ITA Airways flight deals on popular routes, helping travelers manage travel expenses efficiently. The airline is part of the SkyTeam alliance and maintains codeshare partnerships with several global airlines, enabling passengers to access extended worldwide connectivity.

      ITA Airways Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for ITA Airways flights either at airport counters or through online check-in services. Digital check-in options are generally preferred as they provide flexibility and help reduce waiting time at the airport. In certain situations, passengers may be required to complete check-in procedures directly at the airport counter. For any check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must arrive at the airport to complete check-in formalities. For most flights, airport check-in counters typically close around 40 minutes before domestic departures and about 60 minutes before international departures. Passengers should always confirm exact timing before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: ITA Airways online check-in usually opens 48 hours before departure and remains available until approximately 90 minutes prior to scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      ITA Airways Frequent Flyer Program

      Volare is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers through points accumulation and travel benefits. Members can earn points by flying with ITA Airways and partner airlines, as well as through hotels, car rentals, and lifestyle partners connected with the program.
      Accumulated points may be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, and additional travel services.
      The program includes several membership tiers such as Smart, Plus, Premium, and Executive, offering increasing benefits including priority services and extra baggage allowance.

      Baggage Allowance

      ITA Airways Baggage Policy

      ITA Airways baggage allowance varies depending on route, fare family, and travel class. Complimentary checked baggage may be included on selected fares, especially on international routes and premium cabins.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are generally permitted one cabin bag and one personal item at no additional cost. Cabin baggage must not exceed standard airline size limits and typically weighs up to 8 kg. Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags should fit under the seat or in overhead compartments.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage allowance depends on ticket type and destination. Economy fares may include one checked bag on intercontinental routes, while Business Class passengers receive higher baggage limits. Weight and size restrictions apply according to airline guidelines.
      Elite members of the Volare program may receive additional baggage privileges depending on membership level.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline regulations.

      Airline Charges & Cancellation

      Airline Charges

      ITA Airways applies airline charges based on travel class, fare category, destination, and optional services selected during booking. Passengers are generally allowed one cabin bag with a maximum weight of 8 kg along with a personal item, while checked baggage allowances depend on route and ticket type. Economy and Premium Economy fares typically include baggage up to 23 kg, whereas Business Class passengers receive higher limits. Additional fees may apply for excess baggage, seat selection, special assistance services, and airport upgrades. Optional services such as extra baggage or preferred seating are charged per journey and may not always be refundable once used. Taxes, airport surcharges, and government fees are included depending on departure country regulations. Understanding baggage limits and optional service pricing before travel helps passengers avoid unexpected costs and manage their journey efficiently.

      Airline Cancellation

      ITA Airways provides cancellation and refund options according to fare rules and timing of the request. Passengers may cancel eligible tickets within 24 hours of booking for a full refund when reservations are made at least one week before departure. Refundable or flexible fares allow passengers to request cash refunds, while non-refundable tickets may offer travel credits or partial refunds depending on conditions. Flight changes and refund requests can be managed online or through customer support, although service fees may apply when assistance is required.       International Flights & Routes

      International Flights

      ITA Airways operates an expanding international flight network connecting Italy with destinations across Europe, North America, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. The airline primarily operates long-haul services from its main hub at Rome Fiumicino Airport, supported by Milan Linate for regional connectivity. International flights offer multiple cabin experiences including Economy, Premium Economy, and Business Class, with onboard services such as meals, entertainment, and baggage allowances included on most long-haul routes. ITA Airways was established as the successor to Alitalia and continues to strengthen global connectivity through partnerships and cooperation with major airline groups, enabling passengers to access extended worldwide destinations through coordinated schedules and seamless transfers.

      International Routes

      ITA Airways maintains a growing international route structure linking Rome and Milan with major global cities including New York, Boston, Miami, Tokyo, Delhi (seasonally via partnerships), and several European and Mediterranean destinations. The airline regularly adjusts routes based on travel demand, seasonal tourism trends, and international market conditions. Its hub-and-spoke network allows efficient connections between domestic Italian cities and long-haul intercontinental flights. Through codeshare agreements and alliance cooperation, travelers can continue to additional destinations beyond ITA-operated routes using single bookings and coordinated baggage transfers. The airline continues to expand connectivity following strategic partnerships within the European aviation market, improving travel options and global accessibility for both business and leisure passengers.
