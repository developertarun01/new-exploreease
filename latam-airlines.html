      About LATAM Airlines

      LATAM Airlines, formed in 2012 following the merger of LAN Airlines and TAM Airlines, is the largest airline group in Latin America, with main headquarters in Santiago, Chile. The airline connects passengers to more than 140 destinations across South America, North America, Europe, Oceania, and the Caribbean through an extensive regional and international network. LATAM operates major hubs in Santiago, São Paulo, Lima, Bogotá, and Quito, allowing efficient connectivity throughout the continent. Exploreease enables travelers to complete LATAM Airlines flight reservations easily through an online booking platform. Passengers can search routes and book affordable LATAM flight tickets conveniently in one place. We also feature selected LATAM Airlines flight deals on popular routes to help travelers manage overall travel expenses. Through strategic partnerships and codeshare agreements with global airlines, LATAM offers expanded connectivity and access to destinations beyond its own network.

      LATAM Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers flying with LATAM Airlines may complete check-in either at airport counters or through digital check-in services before departure. Online check-in is generally preferred as it allows travelers to save time and obtain boarding passes in advance. Certain travel conditions or documentation requirements may require airport assistance. For help related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Passengers should arrive early to complete airport formalities. For domestic flights, check-in is usually required at least 45 minutes prior to departure, while international travelers are advised to complete check-in 60–90 minutes before departure depending on airport procedures. Exact deadlines should always be verified before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: LATAM online check-in generally opens 48 hours before departure (and earlier for eligible members) and remains available until shortly before scheduled departure through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      LATAM Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      LATAM Pass is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers through point accumulation and redemption opportunities. Members earn LATAM Pass miles when flying with LATAM and partner airlines, as well as through hotels, car rentals, financial partners, and travel services.
      Accumulated miles can be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, and additional travel benefits.
      The program offers multiple elite membership categories, providing benefits such as priority services, additional baggage allowance, and airport privileges.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      LATAM Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance varies according to fare family, travel route, cabin class, and loyalty status. Applicable fees may differ depending on the number of bags and destination.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are typically permitted one carry-on bag along with one personal item without additional charge. The standard carry-on dimensions generally must not exceed 55 cm x 35 cm x 25 cm, including handles and wheels. Personal items should fit beneath the seat in front.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage may be included with certain fare categories and premium cabins, while basic fares may require additional payment. Standard checked baggage must usually not exceed 158 cm (62 inches) in total dimensions with a weight limit commonly up to 23 kg (50 lbs), although Business Class and elite members may receive higher limits. Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline policies.
