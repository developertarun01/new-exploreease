      About Caribbean Airlines

      Caribbean Airlines, established in 2006, is the national airline of Trinidad and Tobago, with its headquarters located in Piarco. The airline operates an extensive regional and international network connecting the Caribbean with North America and South America. Major hubs include Piarco International Airport in Trinidad and Norman Manley International Airport in Jamaica. Exploreease allows travelers to make Caribbean Airlines flight reservations conveniently through an online booking platform. Passengers can easily search and secure competitive ticket options while also accessing selected promotional Caribbean Airlines flight deals on popular routes. The airline focuses on providing warm Caribbean hospitality along with reliable air travel services connecting key regional destinations.

      Baggage Allowance

      Caribbean Airlines Baggage Policy

      Caribbean Airlines baggage allowances vary depending on fare type, travel route, and cabin class.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag and one personal item free of charge. Carry-on baggage must generally not exceed standard cabin size limits and must fit safely in overhead compartments.
      Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit under the seat in front.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on many Caribbean Airlines fares, especially on international routes. Standard checked baggage usually follows a maximum linear dimension of 62 inches and weight limits typically around 50 lbs per bag in Economy Class, while Business Class passengers may receive higher allowances.
      Additional baggage or overweight items may incur extra fees depending on route conditions.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add additional baggage while booking Caribbean Airlines flights through our platform.

      Caribbean Airlines permits itinerary changes depending on the selected fare type, travel route, cabin class, and ticket conditions confirmed at the time of purchase.       Caribbean Airlines offers cancellation policies that vary depending on fare conditions and ticket type.
