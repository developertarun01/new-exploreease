      About Caribbean Airlines

      Caribbean Airlines, established in 2006, is the national airline of Trinidad and Tobago, with headquarters located in Piarco, Trinidad. The airline connects travelers to key destinations across the Caribbean, North America, and South America through a growing regional and international network. Its primary hub is Piarco International Airport in Trinidad, supported by a secondary hub at Norman Manley International Airport in Kingston, Jamaica.
