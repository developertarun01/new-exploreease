      About Qatar Airways

      Qatar Airways, established in 1993 and relaunched in 1997, is the national carrier of the State of Qatar and one of the world’s leading full-service airlines. Headquartered in Doha, the airline operates its global network through Hamad International Airport, connecting travelers to more than 170 destinations across Europe, Asia, Africa, the Americas, and Oceania. Qatar Airways is recognized for premium onboard services and modern aircraft operations. Exploreease allows travelers to complete Qatar Airways flight reservations conveniently through a simple online booking platform. As a member of the oneworld alliance, the airline maintains strong partnerships with global carriers, offering extended connectivity and seamless international travel options.

      Qatar Airways Check-in Methods

      Passengers flying with Qatar Airways may complete check-in either at airport counters or through online check-in services prior to departure. Online check-in is generally recommended as it allows travelers to select seats and receive boarding passes before arriving at the airport. Certain travel documentation checks may require in-person verification. For assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete airport procedures. For international flights, check-in typically closes 60 minutes before departure, and passengers are advised to arrive at least three hours prior to scheduled takeoff depending on airport regulations. Exact deadlines should always be verified before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Qatar Airways online check-in usually opens 48 hours before departure and remains available until 90 minutes before scheduled departure through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Qatar Airways Frequent Flyer Program

      Privilege Club is Qatar Airways’ loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers through Avios points accumulation. Members can earn Avios when flying with Qatar Airways, oneworld partner airlines, and various travel and lifestyle partners.
      Earned Avios may be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, excess baggage, and travel-related services.
      The program includes multiple elite tiers such as Burgundy, Silver, Gold, and Platinum, offering benefits like lounge access, priority services, and additional baggage allowances.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Qatar Airways Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance varies based on fare family, route, and cabin class. Qatar Airways commonly applies a weight or piece concept depending on destination.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Economy Class passengers are generally allowed one carry-on bag within standard cabin dimensions, while Business and First Class passengers may carry additional cabin baggage. Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit beneath the seat.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on most Qatar Airways fares. Economy Class allowances typically range from 20 kg to 35 kg depending on fare type, while Business and First Class passengers receive higher limits. Individual checked bags must usually not exceed 32 kg each according to international safety regulations. Special baggage including sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported subject to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add extra baggage during the Qatar Airways flight booking process through our platform.

      International Flights & Routes

      Qatar Airways International Flights

      Qatar Airways operates one of the world’s most extensive international flight networks, connecting Doha with major cities across six continents. Long-haul services include multiple cabin experiences such as Economy, Business Class featuring Qsuite on select aircraft, and First Class on certain routes. International passengers benefit from premium onboard services including meals, inflight entertainment, Wi-Fi availability, and generous baggage allowances.
