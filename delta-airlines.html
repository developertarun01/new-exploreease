      About Delta Airlines

      Delta Air Lines, founded in 1925, is one of the largest and most recognized airlines in the United States, with its headquarters based in Atlanta, Georgia. The airline operates flights to more than 300 destinations across six continents, covering both domestic and international routes. Delta runs its global operations through several major hubs, with Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport serving as its primary hub. Exploreease enables travelers to book Delta Airlines flight reservations quickly and conveniently online. You can search and secure affordable Delta flight tickets directly through our platform. We also highlight selected Delta Airlines flight deals on popular routes to help travelers save more on their journeys. Delta is a founding member of the SkyTeam airline alliance and maintains extensive codeshare partnerships with numerous international carriers, allowing passengers seamless connectivity to major cities around the world.

      Delta Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers can check in for Delta Airlines flights either at the airport or through convenient digital options. Online check-in is commonly preferred as it reduces waiting time and simplifies the travel process. However, certain travel requirements may require airport check-in. For assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Passengers should arrive at the airport early to complete check-in formalities. For domestic flights, check-in is generally required at least 45 minutes before departure, while international passengers are advised to complete the process at least 60 minutes prior to scheduled departure. Travelers should always verify exact timing requirements before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Delta Airlines online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until shortly before the flight departure time. Passengers can check in through Delta’s official website or mobile application for added convenience.

      Delta Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      SkyMiles is Delta’s loyalty rewards program that allows members to earn miles through flights with Delta and its partner airlines, hotel bookings, vacation packages, car rentals, credit card spending, shopping, and other partner services.
      Earned miles can be redeemed for award travel, seat upgrades, and other travel-related benefits.
      The program features multiple membership tiers including Silver Medallion, Gold Medallion, Platinum Medallion, and Diamond Medallion status levels.

      Baggage Allowance

      Delta Airlines Baggage Policy

      Delta Airlines baggage allowance varies depending on fare type, travel route, cabin class, and frequent flyer status. Complimentary checked baggage may not be included with all fare types.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are allowed one carry-on bag along with one personal item free of charge. Carry-on items must fit within overhead bins or under the seat in front. Standard carry-on size limits generally follow airline safety guidelines.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage fees depend on destination, ticket class, and membership level. Standard checked baggage usually allows up to 62 linear inches and a weight limit of 50 lbs per bag in Economy class, while premium cabins may permit higher weight allowances.
      Medallion members may receive additional baggage benefits based on their status level.
      Special items such as sports equipment and musical instruments may be transported according to Delta Airlines policies.
      Passengers can include checked baggage while booking Delta Airlines flight tickets through our platform.
