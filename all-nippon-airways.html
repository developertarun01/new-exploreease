      About All Nippon Airways

      All Nippon Airways (ANA), established in 1952, is Japan’s largest airline and one of Asia’s leading full-service carriers. Headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, the airline operates an extensive domestic and international network connecting more than 90 destinations worldwide. Major hubs include Tokyo Haneda, Tokyo Narita, Osaka Itami, and Kansai International Airport. Exploreease enables travelers to make All Nippon Airways flight reservations conveniently through an online booking platform. Passengers can easily search and secure competitive ANA ticket options while also accessing selected promotional flight deals on popular routes. ANA is a member of the Star Alliance, allowing travelers to benefit from seamless global connectivity and coordinated travel experiences through partner airlines across multiple continents.

      Services and Amenities

      All Nippon Airways Check-in Methods

      Passengers may check in for All Nippon Airways flights either at airport counters or through online and mobile check-in services. Online check-in is generally recommended as it simplifies boarding procedures and reduces waiting time at the airport. Certain international travel requirements may still require airport verification. For any assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must complete check-in procedures at the airport within the specified time limits. Domestic flights typically require check-in completion at least 30–45 minutes before departure, while international flights usually require earlier arrival depending on destination regulations.
      2. Online Check-in: ANA online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until shortly before scheduled takeoff through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      All Nippon Airways Frequent Flyer Program

      ANA Mileage Club is the airline’s loyalty rewards program designed to allow members to earn and redeem miles across flights and partner services. Travelers can accumulate miles by flying with ANA or Star Alliance partner airlines, as well as through hotels, car rentals, shopping partners, and co-branded credit cards.
      Miles may be redeemed for award tickets, seat upgrades, or additional travel benefits.
      The program offers multiple membership tiers, including Bronze, Platinum, and Diamond status levels with added privileges.

      Baggage Allowance

      All Nippon Airways Baggage Policy

      All Nippon Airways baggage allowances depend on route type, cabin class, and ANA Mileage Club status.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag along with one personal item free of charge. Carry-on baggage must generally not exceed combined dimensions of 115 cm and a weight limit of 10 kg.
      Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit under the seat in front.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is typically included on most ANA fares. Allowances vary by cabin class, with Economy passengers commonly allowed at least one checked bag, while Premium Economy, Business, and First Class travelers receive higher allowances.
      Standard checked baggage weight limits generally range from 23 kg in Economy Class to 32 kg in premium cabins.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline policies.
      Passengers may add additional baggage while booking ANA flights through our platform.

      Airline Charges

      All Nippon Airways applies charges based on fare type, travel route, cabin selection, and optional services selected by passengers. International fares often include checked baggage allowances depending on cabin class.       All Nippon Airways offers cancellation policies that vary depending on fare conditions and ticket type. Reservations canceled within 24 hours of booking may qualify for refunds when eligible under fare rules. Passengers should always review ticket conditions carefully before canceling to understand applicable policies.
