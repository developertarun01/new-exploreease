      About WestJet

      WestJet, founded in 1996 and headquartered in Calgary, Alberta, is one of Canada’s leading airlines providing both domestic and international air travel. The airline connects passengers to more than 100 destinations across Canada, the United States, Mexico, the Caribbean, Europe, and select transatlantic markets. WestJet operates major hubs in Calgary, Toronto, and Vancouver, offering strong connectivity across North America and beyond.       WestJet Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance depends on fare bundle, destination, and cabin class selected during booking. Applicable fees may vary depending on travel conditions.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are generally permitted one carry-on bag along with one personal item at no additional cost. Cabin baggage must meet airline size requirements and fit safely within overhead compartments or beneath the seat.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage may be included with certain fare bundles and premium cabins, while basic fares may require additional payment. Standard checked baggage typically allows up to 23 kg (50 lbs) per bag with maximum linear dimensions of 158 cm (62 inches). Higher allowances may apply for premium cabins or eligible loyalty members. Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline policies.
