      About Turkish Airlines

      Turkish Airlines, established in 1933, is the national flag carrier of Türkiye and one of the world’s largest international airlines by destination coverage. Headquartered in Istanbul, the airline operates its global network primarily from Istanbul Airport, connecting passengers to more than 300 destinations across Europe, Asia, Africa, the Americas, and the Middle East. Known for its extensive route network and full-service travel experience, Turkish Airlines provides strong connectivity between continents through its strategic hub location. Exploreease allows travelers to complete Turkish Airlines flight reservations conveniently through an easy online booking platform. Passengers can search routes and book affordable Turkish Airlines flight tickets directly on our website. We also feature selected Turkish Airlines flight deals on major international routes, helping travelers manage travel expenses efficiently. As a member of the Star Alliance network, Turkish Airlines offers expanded connectivity through numerous partner airlines worldwide.

      Turkish Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers traveling with Turkish Airlines may complete check-in either at airport counters or through online check-in services before departure. Online check-in is generally recommended as it allows travelers to select seats and receive boarding passes in advance. Certain routes may require document verification at airport desks. For assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete airport procedures. For international flights, check-in typically closes about 60 minutes before departure, and passengers are advised to arrive at least three hours before takeoff depending on airport regulations. Exact deadlines should always be confirmed prior to travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Turkish Airlines online check-in usually opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until approximately 90 minutes before scheduled departure through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Turkish Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      Miles&Smiles is Turkish Airlines’ loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers through mileage earning and redemption opportunities. Members earn miles when flying with Turkish Airlines, Star Alliance partners, and affiliated travel or lifestyle partners.
      Miles may be redeemed for award tickets, cabin upgrades, extra baggage, and additional travel benefits.
      The program includes multiple elite membership tiers such as Classic, Elite, and Elite Plus, offering privileges like lounge access, priority services, and additional baggage allowances.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Turkish Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance varies depending on fare type, destination, and cabin class, with either piece or weight concepts applied on different routes.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Economy Class passengers are generally allowed one cabin bag along with one personal item within standard size limits, while Business Class passengers may carry additional cabin baggage. Personal belongings must fit safely beneath the seat or in overhead compartments.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on most Turkish Airlines fares. Economy Class allowances commonly range around 23 kg per piece, while Business Class passengers receive higher limits depending on route conditions. Individual checked bags typically must not exceed 32 kg due to safety regulations. Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add additional checked baggage while booking Turkish Airlines flight tickets through our platform.

      Turkish Airlines offers cancellation flexibility depending on fare conditions and applicable travel regulations.       International Flights & Routes

      Turkish Airlines International Flights

      Turkish Airlines operates one of the most extensive international flight networks in the aviation industry, connecting Istanbul with major cities across six continents. Long-haul services provide multiple cabin options including Economy and Business Class designed for comfort on intercontinental journeys. International passengers benefit from onboard meals, entertainment systems, and generous baggage allowances compared to regional travel. Through Star Alliance partnerships, Turkish Airlines enables seamless global connections and coordinated travel experiences worldwide.

      Turkish Airlines International Routes

      Turkish Airlines maintains a vast international route structure linking Istanbul with destinations such as London, Paris, New York, Toronto, Dubai, Delhi, Bangkok, Johannesburg, and Sydney via connecting services. The airline frequently adjusts routes based on seasonal demand and operational planning while strengthening connectivity through alliance partners. Many schedules are optimized for efficient transfers through Istanbul Airport, allowing passengers to continue onward journeys smoothly.
