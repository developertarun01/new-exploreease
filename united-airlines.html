      About United Airlines

      United Airlines, established in 1926, is one of the leading airlines in the United States, with headquarters located in Chicago, Illinois. The airline serves more than 300 destinations worldwide across domestic and international routes. It operates through seven major hubs, with Chicago O’Hare being the largest. Exploreease allows travelers to make United Airlines flight reservations conveniently online. You can easily search and book affordable United flight tickets through our platform. We also feature selected United Airlines flight deals on specific routes, helping travelers reduce overall travel expenses. The airline is a founding member of Star Alliance and maintains codeshare partnerships with over 30 global airlines, enabling passengers to access flights to major destinations worldwide.

      Services & Amenities

      United Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for United Airlines flights either at the airport counter or through online options. In most cases, online check-in is preferred because it offers greater convenience and saves time. However, certain situations require passengers to check in directly at the airport. For any check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must arrive at the airport to complete check-in procedures. For domestic flights, check-in should be finalized at least 45 minutes before departure, while international travelers are advised to complete the process at least 60–90 minutes depending on airport requirements. These timings are general guidelines, and passengers should verify exact deadlines before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: United Airlines online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until 1 hour before scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      United Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      MileagePlus is the airline’s loyalty rewards program designed around earning and redeeming mileage points. Members can accumulate miles through flights with United and partner airlines, hotel stays, vacation bookings, car rentals, credit card usage, shopping, and financial partners connected with the airline.
      These miles may later be redeemed for award travel or cabin upgrades.
      The program offers four membership tiers: Silver, Gold, Platinum, and 1K.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      United Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage inclusion depends on fare type and route. Fees vary based on the number of bags, fare type, frequent flyer level, cabin class, and route.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag and one personal item at no additional cost. The maximum carry-on dimensions allowed are 9 inches (22 cm) x 14 inches (35 cm) x 22 inches (56 cm).
      Personal items must not exceed 9 inches (22 cm) x 10 inches (25 cm) x 17 inches (43 cm).
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is typically not included. Travelers may carry up to two checked bags with maximum linear dimensions of 62 inches and weight up to 51 lbs each. Weight limits may vary depending on travel class and membership status. United Economy allows up to 50 lbs, while premium cabins may allow up to 70 lbs per bag.
      Premier Silver, Gold, Platinum, and 1K members may also carry bags weighing up to 70 lbs each.
      Special baggage such as sports gear or musical instruments may be transported according to airline rules.
      Passengers can add checked baggage while booking United flight tickets through our platform.

      United Airlines Flight Booking

      United Airlines offers a modern and user-friendly flight booking experience through its official website, allowing travelers to search, compare, and reserve flights to more than 300 global destinations with ease. The platform features an intuitive flight search system where users can select travel dates, cabin classes, and fare options while viewing transparent pricing and schedules. Passengers can also manage bookings online, choose preferred seats, add travel extras, and pay using cash or MileagePlus miles. The website supports online check-in starting 24 hours before departure and provides real-time trip updates through integrated digital tools and the United mobile app. Travelers can access baggage information, travel requirements, airport guidance, and upgrade options in one place, making trip planning convenient and efficient. With personalized services, flexible booking management, and additional options like priority boarding and seat upgrades, United Airlines ensures a smooth and reliable booking journey from planning to boarding.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      United Airlines Flight Changes

      United Airlines allows itinerary modifications based on fare type, travel route, cabin class, and ticket conditions selected during booking. Most Economy, Economy Plus, and premium cabin fares permit passengers to change travel dates, flight times, or destinations through the manage reservation feature, although Basic Economy tickets may have more limited flexibility. Same-day confirmed changes may be available on eligible flights for a fee or without charge for qualifying MileagePlus elite members. Voluntary updates remain subject to seat availability and applicable fare rules. When a booking is modified, travelers must pay any difference in airfare, taxes, or carrier-imposed surcharges if the new flight costs more. International reservations may follow specific change policies depending on fare class. Reviewing ticket terms before requesting updates helps passengers manage travel plans smoothly and avoid unexpected complications.

      United Airlines Flight Cancellation

      United Airlines offers flexible cancellation policies designed to give passengers more control over travel plans. Tickets can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund when the booking is made at least seven days before departure. Refundable fares qualify for cash refunds, while most non-refundable tickets typically provide future flight credits instead of direct refunds. United has removed change and cancellation fees for most standard economy and premium tickets, although Basic Economy fares may still have restrictions. If the airline cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers are eligible for refunds or rebooking options. Travel credits issued after voluntary cancellations usually remain valid for a limited period, allowing customers to reschedule later. Policies may vary slightly for international itineraries, fare brands, or special promotional tickets, so reviewing ticket conditions remains important before canceling.

      International Flights & Routes

      United Airlines International Flights

      United Airlines operates one of the largest international flight networks among U.S. carriers, connecting travelers from major hubs such as Chicago, Newark, San Francisco, Houston, Washington D.C., and Los Angeles to destinations worldwide. The airline provides long-haul services across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Oceania, and Latin America, offering multiple cabin options including Economy, Premium Economy, Business, and Polaris Business Class for enhanced comfort on intercontinental journeys. International flights often include additional onboard services such as meals, entertainment systems, and baggage allowances compared to domestic travel. United is also a founding member of the Star Alliance, allowing passengers to access extended global connectivity through partner airlines and seamless transfers across continents. This global cooperation enables travelers to reach hundreds of cities worldwide using coordinated schedules and shared loyalty benefits.

      United Airlines International Routes

      United Airlines maintains an extensive international route structure linking North America with major global destinations. Popular routes include nonstop flights from U.S. hubs to cities such as London, Frankfurt, Paris, Tokyo, Delhi, Sydney, Dubai (via partners), and numerous destinations across Canada, Mexico, and South America. The airline continually adjusts routes based on travel demand, seasonal tourism trends, and operational efficiency while expanding long-haul connectivity through partner networks. Many international services are designed to support both business and leisure travelers, with optimized schedules for global connections. Through Star Alliance partnerships, passengers can continue onward journeys to smaller regional destinations without separate bookings. This integrated routing system strengthens United’s presence across six continents and allows travelers to plan complex international itineraries with coordinated baggage handling, shared lounges, and synchronized flight connections.
