      About United Airlines

      United Airlines, established in 1926, is one of the leading airlines in the United States, with headquarters located in Chicago, Illinois. The airline serves nearly 350 destinations across domestic and international routes. It operates through eight major hubs, with Chicago O’Hare being the largest. Exploreease allows travelers to make United Airlines flight reservations conveniently online. You can easily search and book affordable United flight tickets through our platform. We also feature selected United Airlines flight deals on specific routes, helping travelers reduce overall travel expenses. The airline is a founding member of Star Alliance and maintains codeshare partnerships with over 30 global airlines, enabling passengers to access flights to major destinations worldwide.

      United Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for United Airlines flights either at the airport counter or through online options. In most cases, online check-in is preferred because it offers greater convenience and saves time. However, certain situations require passengers to check in directly at the airport. For any check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must arrive at the airport to complete check-in procedures. For domestic flights, check-in should be finalized at least 45 minutes before departure, while international travelers are advised to complete the process at least 60 minutes prior. These timings are general guidelines, and passengers should verify exact deadlines before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: United Airlines online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until 1 hour before scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      United Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      MileagePlus is the airline’s loyalty rewards program designed around earning and redeeming mileage points. Members can accumulate miles through flights with United and partner airlines, hotel stays, vacation bookings, car rentals, credit card usage, shopping, and financial partners connected with the airline.
      These miles may later be redeemed for award travel or cabin upgrades.
      The program offers four membership tiers: Silver, Gold, Platinum, and 1K.

      Baggage Allowance

      United Airlines Baggage Policy

      United Airlines generally does not include complimentary checked baggage. Fees vary based on the number of bags, fare type, frequent flyer level, cabin class, and route.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag and one personal item at no additional cost. The maximum carry-on dimensions allowed are 9 inches (22 cm) x 14 inches (35 cm) x 22 inches (56 cm).
      Personal items must not exceed 9 inches (22 cm) x 10 inches (25 cm) x 17 inches (43 cm).
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is typically not included. Travelers may carry up to two checked bags with maximum linear dimensions of 62 inches and weight up to 51 lbs each. Weight limits may vary depending on travel class and membership status. United Economy allows up to 50 lbs, while premium cabins may allow up to 70 lbs per bag.
      Premier Silver, Gold, Platinum, and 1K members may also carry bags weighing up to 70 lbs each.
      Special baggage such as sports gear or musical instruments may be transported according to airline rules.
      Passengers can add checked baggage while booking United flight tickets through our platform.
