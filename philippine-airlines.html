      About Philippine Airlines

      Philippine Airlines, commonly known as PAL, was established in 1941 and is the national flag carrier of the Philippines. Headquartered in Pasay City near Manila, the airline operates an extensive network connecting more than 60 destinations across Asia, North America, Australia, the Middle East, and domestic Philippine routes. Its primary hub is Ninoy Aquino International Airport in Manila, supported by key operations in Cebu, Clark, and Davao. Exploreease allows travelers to complete Philippine Airlines flight reservations conveniently through an easy online booking system. Through interline and codeshare partnerships with international carriers, the airline provides broader global connectivity and seamless travel options beyond its own network.

      Philippine Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers traveling with Philippine Airlines may complete check-in either at airport counters or through online check-in services prior to departure. Online check-in is generally recommended as it reduces waiting time and allows passengers to obtain boarding passes early. Some international travel requirements may still require verification at the airport. For assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete airport formalities. Domestic passengers are usually required to check in at least 45 minutes before departure, while international travelers are advised to finalize check-in 60–90 minutes prior to departure depending on airport procedures. Exact timing requirements should always be confirmed before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Philippine Airlines online check-in typically opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until a few hours before scheduled takeoff through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Philippine Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      Mabuhay Miles is Philippine Airlines’ loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers with mileage earning opportunities. Members can accumulate miles when flying with Philippine Airlines and partner carriers, as well as through hotels, travel services, car rentals, and financial partners.
      Earned miles may be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, and additional travel benefits.
      The program offers several membership tiers, providing advantages such as priority services, lounge access, and additional baggage privileges.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Philippine Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance depends on fare type, travel route, and cabin class selected during booking. Policies may vary between domestic and international flights.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are generally permitted one carry-on bag along with one personal item at no additional cost. The carry-on bag should typically not exceed 56 cm x 36 cm x 23 cm including handles and wheels, while personal items must fit beneath the seat.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on many Philippine Airlines fares, particularly on international routes. Standard checked baggage usually allows up to 23 kg (50 lbs) per piece in Economy Class, while Business Class passengers may receive higher allowances depending on route and fare conditions. Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported subject to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add additional checked baggage during the Philippine Airlines flight booking process through our platform.
