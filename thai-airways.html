      About Thai Airways

      Thai Airways International, founded in 1961, is the national flag carrier of Thailand, with headquarters located in Bangkok. The airline connects passengers to major destinations across Asia, Europe, Australia, and other international regions through an extensive global network. Its primary hub is Suvarnabhumi Airport in Bangkok, serving as a key gateway for international travel. Exploreease allows travelers to make Thai Airways flight reservations easily through a convenient online platform. The airline is a founding member of Star Alliance and maintains partnerships with global carriers, enabling smooth connections to destinations worldwide.

      Services and Amenities

      Thai Airways Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for Thai Airways flights either at airport counters or through digital check-in services. Online and mobile check-in options are commonly preferred as they reduce waiting time and provide added convenience before departure. In certain cases, passengers may still need to complete procedures directly at the airport counter. For any check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must present travel documents at the airport counter to complete check-in. For international flights, counters generally close about 50–60 minutes before departure, while domestic flights usually close around 40 minutes prior. Passengers are advised to confirm exact timelines before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Thai Airways online check-in opens approximately 24 hours before departure and remains available until about 1 hour prior to scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Thai Airways Frequent Flyer Program

      Royal Orchid Plus is the airline’s frequent flyer program designed to reward loyal travelers through mileage earning and redemption benefits. Members can collect miles when flying with Thai Airways or Star Alliance partners, along with hotel stays, travel services, and lifestyle partners connected to the program.
      Miles earned can later be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, lounge access, and other travel privileges.
      The program offers multiple membership levels including Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers, providing additional baggage allowance and priority travel benefits.

      Baggage Allowance

      Thai Airways Baggage Policy

      Thai Airways generally includes complimentary checked baggage depending on travel class, route, and fare conditions. Allowance may follow either the weight or piece concept based on destination regulations.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one cabin bag along with one personal item at no extra cost. Cabin baggage must fit within airline size limits and typically weigh up to 7 kg in Economy Class, while premium cabins may allow additional allowance.
      Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit under the seat or within overhead compartments.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on most international fares. Allowance varies by cabin class, with higher limits provided for premium cabins. Royal Orchid Plus Gold and Platinum members may receive additional baggage privileges depending on membership level.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline regulations.
      Passengers can add or manage baggage while booking Thai Airways flight tickets through our platform.
