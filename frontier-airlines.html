      About Frontier Airlines

      Frontier Airlines, founded in 1994, is a U.S.-based ultra-low-cost carrier headquartered in Denver, Colorado. The airline focuses on providing affordable air travel across the United States as well as select international destinations in Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central America. Operating primarily from Denver International Airport along with several focus cities, Frontier Airlines serves more than 100 destinations through a growing route network. Exploreease allows travelers to make Frontier Airlines flight reservations easily through a convenient online process. Passengers can search schedules, compare fares, and book budget-friendly Frontier flight tickets directly on our platform. We also feature selected Frontier Airlines flight deals on popular routes, helping travelers minimize travel expenses. The airline operates a modern fleet of Airbus aircraft and follows an à-la-carte pricing model, allowing passengers to customize services according to individual travel needs.

      Services & Amenities

      Frontier Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers traveling with Frontier Airlines may complete check-in either online or at airport kiosks and ticket counters. Online check-in is generally recommended because it helps travelers avoid additional airport service fees and reduces waiting time. Certain situations, including special service requests or document verification, may require airport assistance. For any check-in or boarding support, travelers may contact Exploreease customer service.
      1. Airport Check-in: Passengers should arrive early to complete airport formalities. For most domestic flights, check-in typically closes about 45 minutes before departure, while international travelers should allow additional time depending on airport procedures.
      2. Online Check-in: Frontier Airlines online check-in usually opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until approximately 60 minutes prior to scheduled takeoff. Travelers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Frontier Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      FRONTIER Miles is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward frequent travelers with mileage earnings based on distance flown and qualifying purchases. Members can earn miles through flights, partner services, and co-branded credit card spending.
      Accumulated miles may be redeemed for award flights, seat upgrades, or travel benefits.
      The program includes elite status levels such as Elite Silver, Elite Gold, and Elite Platinum, offering benefits like priority boarding and additional perks.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Frontier Airlines Baggage Policy

      Baggage allowances on Frontier Airlines depend on the fare bundle selected during booking. As an ultra-low-cost carrier, most fares include only a personal item, while additional baggage options may be purchased separately.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one personal item free of charge, typically not exceeding 14 inches x 18 inches x 8 inches. Standard carry-on bags require an additional fee unless included in a bundled fare.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage is generally not included in base fares and must be added separately. Travelers may purchase checked bags in advance online at lower prices compared to airport purchases. Weight limits usually allow up to 40 lbs (18 kg) per checked bag, with size restrictions based on airline guidelines.
      Oversized or special baggage such as sports equipment may be transported according to airline policies.
      Passengers can add baggage while booking Frontier Airlines flight tickets through our platform.

      Frontier Airlines Flight Booking

      Frontier Airlines provides a straightforward online booking system designed for travelers seeking low fares and flexible add-on options. Through its official website, passengers can search flights, compare pricing, and customize travel by selecting seats, baggage, and onboard services based on personal preferences. The booking platform displays transparent pricing, allowing customers to control costs by choosing only the services they need. Travelers can manage bookings online, update passenger details, and purchase extras such as priority boarding or seat assignments. Online check-in opens before departure, and trip updates are available through digital notifications and the Frontier mobile app. With its budget-focused structure and customizable travel features, Frontier Airlines offers a simple and efficient booking experience from planning to boarding.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Frontier Airlines Flight Changes

      Frontier Airlines allows itinerary changes depending on fare type and ticket conditions selected during booking. Passengers may modify travel dates, flight times, or destinations through the manage booking section online. Change fees may apply depending on how close the modification is made to departure, although certain fare bundles may provide greater flexibility. Any fare difference between the original and new flight must be paid if applicable. All updates are subject to seat availability and airline policies. Reviewing fare rules before requesting changes helps travelers manage plans efficiently and avoid additional costs.

      Frontier Airlines Flight Cancellation

      Frontier Airlines offers cancellation options based on fare rules and purchase conditions. Tickets may be canceled within 24 hours of booking for a full refund when reservations are made at least seven days before departure. Most non-refundable fares are issued as travel credits rather than cash refunds after cancellation. If the airline cancels a flight or significantly changes the schedule, passengers may choose refunds or alternative travel arrangements. Travel credits generally remain valid for a limited period, allowing customers to rebook later. Policies may vary depending on promotional fares or bundled packages, so reviewing ticket terms before canceling is recommended.

      International Flights & Routes

      Frontier Airlines International Flights

      Frontier Airlines operates international services primarily to leisure destinations across Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central America. Flights are designed to provide affordable travel options with a simplified onboard experience focused on efficiency and value. While onboard amenities are more basic compared with full-service carriers, passengers may purchase snacks, beverages, and comfort upgrades during the flight. The airline’s international operations support vacation travel with direct connections from major U.S. cities to popular tourist destinations.

      Frontier Airlines International Routes

      Frontier Airlines maintains a growing route network connecting major U.S. cities such as Denver, Orlando, Las Vegas, Atlanta, and Philadelphia with destinations including Cancun, Punta Cana, San Juan, Montego Bay, and other leisure markets. Routes are adjusted frequently based on seasonal demand and tourism trends. The airline’s scheduling strategy focuses on point-to-point travel, allowing passengers to reach vacation destinations directly at competitive prices. This flexible network structure helps Frontier expand service while maintaining its low-cost operating model and accessible travel options.
