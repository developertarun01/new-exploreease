      About Frontier Airlines

      Frontier Airlines, founded in 1994 and headquartered in Denver, Colorado, is an American ultra-low-cost carrier known for offering affordable travel options across the United States and select international destinations. The airline operates a growing network serving more than 100 cities throughout the U.S., Mexico, Central America, and the Caribbean, with Denver International Airport serving as its primary hub. Exploreease allows travelers to make Frontier Airlines flight reservations easily through a convenient online booking platform. Passengers can quickly search and secure budget-friendly Frontier Airlines tickets while accessing selected promotional deals on popular routes. Frontier follows a low-fare model that allows travelers to customize their journey by paying only for optional services they choose.

      Services and Amenities

      Frontier Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers may check in for Frontier Airlines flights through online check-in, mobile boarding passes, airport kiosks, or ticket counters. Online check-in is generally recommended because it helps avoid airport service fees and reduces waiting time. Some travelers may still require airport assistance depending on travel requirements. For any help related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete check-in procedures. Airport check-in typically closes about 60 minutes before departure for domestic flights, although timing may vary by airport.
      2. Online Check-in: Frontier Airlines online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until shortly before scheduled takeoff through the airline’s website or mobile application.

      Frontier Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      FRONTIER Miles is the airline’s loyalty program designed to reward travelers for flights and partner purchases. Members earn miles when flying with Frontier Airlines as well as through hotels, car rentals, and co-branded credit card spending.
      Miles can be redeemed for award flights, seat options, and other travel-related benefits.
      The program includes elite status levels such as Elite Silver, Elite Gold, and Elite Diamond, offering additional perks like priority boarding and seat selection benefits.

      Baggage Allowance

      Frontier Airlines Baggage Policy

      Frontier Airlines follows an à-la-carte baggage structure where most optional services are purchased separately depending on traveler preferences.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: A personal item that fits under the seat is included with every ticket at no additional charge. Standard carry-on bags require an additional fee unless included in bundled fare options. Carry-on dimensions typically must not exceed 24 inches x 16 inches x 10 inches.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage is not included in basic fares and must be purchased separately. Standard checked bags usually follow a maximum linear dimension of 62 inches with a weight limit of 50 lbs.
      Additional fees may apply for overweight or oversized baggage.
      Special items such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add baggage while booking Frontier Airlines flights through our platform.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Airline Changes

      Frontier Airlines allows itinerary modifications depending on the selected fare, travel dates, and ticket conditions confirmed at the time of booking. Most reservations can be updated online through the manage my trip section, though flexibility varies based on the purchased fare and any added bundle options. Passengers may change flight dates, times, or destinations subject to availability on alternative flights. Changes made without flexible fare options may include applicable modification fees along with any increase in airfare. Travelers who select bundled packages with added flexibility may receive reduced change charges within permitted timelines. Same-day adjustments depend on seat availability and current fare rules. When modifying a reservation, any difference in fare, taxes, or related surcharges must be paid. Reviewing ticket terms beforehand helps passengers adjust travel plans smoothly and avoid unexpected issues.

      Airline Cancellation

      Frontier Airlines offers cancellation policies that vary depending on fare type and purchased options. Tickets canceled within 24 hours of booking are generally eligible for a full refund when purchased at least seven days before departure. Most basic fares are non-refundable, although travelers may receive travel credits after applicable fees. Customers who select flexible fare options during booking may change or cancel flights with reduced penalties. If Frontier Airlines cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers may request refunds or alternative travel arrangements. Policies may vary for promotional fares, so reviewing ticket conditions before canceling is recommended.

      International Flights & Routes

      International Flights

      Frontier Airlines operates domestic and limited international services connecting the United States with destinations in Mexico, Central America, and the Caribbean. The airline focuses on providing affordable nonstop travel between major cities and leisure destinations. Onboard services follow a simplified low-cost model, with optional purchases available for snacks, beverages, and added comfort features depending on passenger preference.

      International Routes

      Frontier Airlines maintains a growing international route network linking U.S. cities with popular vacation destinations such as Cancun, Punta Cana, San Juan, and various locations across Mexico and the Caribbean. Routes are adjusted seasonally based on travel demand and operational planning. The airline’s point-to-point network allows travelers to reach leisure destinations directly without relying heavily on connecting hubs, helping reduce travel time and overall trip costs.
