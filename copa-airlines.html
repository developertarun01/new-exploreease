      About Copa Airlines

      Copa Airlines, founded in 1947 and headquartered in Panama City, Panama, is the national airline of Panama and one of Latin America’s leading carriers. The airline operates an extensive network connecting North America, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean through its primary hub at Tocumen International Airport, widely known as the “Hub of the Americas.” Exploreease allows travelers to make Copa Airlines flight reservations conveniently through an online booking platform. Passengers can easily search and secure competitive ticket options while also accessing selected promotional Copa Airlines flight deals on popular routes. Copa Airlines is a member of the Star Alliance, enabling seamless global connectivity and coordinated travel experiences through partner airlines worldwide.

      Services and Amenities

      Copa Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers may check in for Copa Airlines flights either at airport counters, self-service kiosks, or through online and mobile check-in services. Online check-in is generally recommended because it saves time and streamlines the boarding process. Certain international itineraries may require document verification at the airport. For assistance related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must arrive early to complete check-in procedures. Domestic and regional flights typically require check-in completion at least 60 minutes before departure, while international flights may require earlier processing depending on destination regulations.
      2. Online Check-in: Copa Airlines online check-in usually opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until approximately 1 hour prior to scheduled takeoff through the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Copa Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      ConnectMiles is Copa Airlines’ loyalty rewards program designed to reward frequent travelers with mileage earning and redemption opportunities. Members can earn miles when flying with Copa Airlines and Star Alliance partner airlines, as well as through hotels, car rentals, financial partners, and promotional offers.
      Miles may be redeemed for award travel, upgrades, and additional travel benefits.
      The program includes multiple elite tiers such as Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Presidential status levels offering added privileges and priority services.

      Baggage Allowance

      Copa Airlines Baggage Policy

      Copa Airlines baggage allowances vary depending on fare type, travel route, cabin class, and ConnectMiles elite status.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag and one personal item free of charge. Carry-on baggage must generally not exceed 22 inches x 14 inches x 10 inches and must fit safely in overhead compartments.
      Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit under the seat in front.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is included on many international fares. Standard checked baggage typically follows a maximum linear dimension of 62 inches and a weight limit of 50 lbs per bag in Economy Class, while Business Class passengers may receive higher weight allowances.
      Elite status members may qualify for additional baggage benefits depending on membership tier.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline policies.
      Passengers may add additional baggage while booking Copa Airlines flights through our platform.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Airline Changes

      Copa Airlines allows flight modifications based on the purchased fare family, travel route, cabin class, and ticket conditions selected during booking. Many fares permit passengers to update travel dates, flight schedules, or destinations through the manage reservation option, although flexibility varies between promotional and flexible fare categories. Standard onboard services remain included according to fare rules, while itinerary adjustments are subject to seat availability on the newly selected flight. Change fees may apply depending on the fare type, and travelers must pay any difference in fare, taxes, or applicable surcharges when modifying bookings. Certain fare bundles may provide greater flexibility for voluntary changes. Reviewing ticket conditions in advance helps passengers make informed decisions and manage travel plans more efficiently.

      Airline Cancellation

      Copa Airlines offers cancellation policies that vary depending on ticket conditions and fare type. Reservations canceled within 24 hours of booking may qualify for refunds when eligible under fare rules. Refundable fares generally allow cash refunds, while restricted fares may provide travel credits or partial refunds after applicable deductions. If Copa Airlines cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers may request refunds or alternative travel arrangements based on eligibility. Travel credits issued after voluntary cancellations typically remain valid for a limited period, allowing travelers to reschedule future trips. Passengers should review ticket terms carefully before canceling.

      International Flights & Routes

      International Flights

      Copa Airlines operates a strong international network connecting major cities across North, Central, and South America as well as the Caribbean through its Panama City hub. The airline offers Economy and Business Class cabins designed for comfort on regional and medium-haul routes. International flights typically include onboard meals, entertainment options, and baggage allowances depending on fare class. As a Star Alliance member, Copa Airlines provides seamless connectivity with partner airlines, enabling travelers to access destinations worldwide with coordinated connections.

      International Routes

      Copa Airlines maintains an extensive route structure linking Panama with major destinations such as Miami, New York, Los Angeles, Toronto, Mexico City, Bogotá, Lima, Santiago, Buenos Aires, and numerous Caribbean cities. Routes are optimized to support efficient connections through the Hub of the Americas, allowing passengers to travel between regions with minimal layover times. Through Star Alliance partnerships, travelers can continue onward journeys to hundreds of additional destinations worldwide using coordinated schedules, shared lounges, and integrated baggage handling for a smoother travel experience.
