      About Copa Airlines

      Copa Airlines, founded in 1947, is the flag carrier of Panama and one of the leading airlines in Latin America, with headquarters located in Panama City, Panama. The airline connects travelers to more than 80 destinations across North America, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean through its extensive hub at Tocumen International Airport, widely known as the “Hub of the Americas.” Exploreease allows travelers to conveniently complete Copa Airlines flight reservations online. Passengers can easily search and book competitive Copa Airlines flight tickets through our platform. We also feature selected Copa Airlines flight deals on major regional routes, helping travelers reduce overall travel expenses. Copa Airlines is a member of the Star Alliance network and maintains codeshare partnerships with multiple global airlines, enabling seamless connectivity to destinations worldwide.

      Services & Amenities

      Copa Airlines Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for Copa Airlines flights either at airport counters, self-service kiosks, or through online check-in services. Online check-in is commonly preferred because it offers convenience and helps reduce waiting time at the airport, although certain itineraries may require document verification in person. For check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete airport procedures. For most international flights, check-in generally closes about 60 minutes before departure depending on airport requirements. These timings are general guidelines, and passengers should confirm exact deadlines prior to travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Copa Airlines online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and is available through the airline’s official website or mobile application, allowing passengers to select seats and receive digital boarding passes.

      Copa Airlines Frequent Flyer Program

      ConnectMiles is Copa Airlines’ loyalty rewards program designed to help members earn and redeem miles through flights and partner services. Members can accumulate miles by flying with Copa Airlines, Star Alliance partner airlines, hotel stays, car rentals, and purchases made with affiliated financial partners.
      Miles earned may be redeemed for award tickets, cabin upgrades, and additional travel benefits.
      The program offers elite tiers including Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Presidential, providing enhanced privileges and priority services for frequent travelers.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Copa Airlines Baggage Policy

      Checked baggage allowance depends on fare family, route, cabin class, and ConnectMiles membership level. Baggage rules may vary between regional and long-haul international routes.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are generally permitted one carry-on bag and one personal item free of charge. The carry-on item must not exceed 22 x 14 x 10 inches including wheels and handles.
      Personal items should fit beneath the seat in front of the passenger.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage is usually included on many fares depending on destination. Standard checked bags typically must not exceed 62 linear inches and generally have a maximum weight allowance of 23 kg in Economy Class, while Business Class may allow heavier baggage limits.
      ConnectMiles elite members may receive additional baggage privileges based on status level.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline policies.
      Passengers can add checked baggage while booking Copa Airlines flight tickets through our platform.

The platform enables passengers to choose travel dates, fare families, and cabin classes while viewing transparent pricing and schedules.       Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Copa Airlines Flight Changes

      Copa Airlines allows itinerary changes depending on fare family, travel route, cabin class, and ticket conditions selected during booking. Many fares permit passengers to modify travel dates, flight times, or destinations through the manage reservation feature, while promotional fares may include certain restrictions. Changes are subject to seat availability and applicable fare rules. When modifying a reservation, travelers may need to pay any difference in airfare, taxes, or surcharges if the replacement flight costs more. International bookings may follow specific change policies depending on fare category. Reviewing ticket rules before requesting updates helps passengers manage travel plans efficiently and avoid unexpected charges.

      Copa Airlines Flight Cancellation

      Copa Airlines provides cancellation options designed to offer flexibility when travel plans change. Tickets canceled within 24 hours of purchase may qualify for a full refund when booked well in advance of departure. Refundable fares are generally eligible for monetary refunds, while most non-refundable tickets may provide future travel credits instead of direct refunds. Cancellation fees and eligibility vary depending on fare type and route. If Copa Airlines cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers may request refunds or alternative travel arrangements. Travel credits issued after voluntary cancellations usually remain valid for a limited period, allowing travelers to rebook later. Reviewing fare conditions before canceling is recommended to avoid unexpected limitations.

      International Flights & Routes

      Copa Airlines International Flights

      Copa Airlines operates an extensive international network connecting travelers through Panama City’s Hub of the Americas to destinations across North, Central, and South America, as well as the Caribbean. The airline offers Economy and Business Class cabins designed for comfort on regional and medium-haul international journeys. International flights typically include onboard meals, entertainment options, and baggage allowances depending on travel class and route length. As a member of the Star Alliance, Copa Airlines provides expanded global connectivity through partner carriers, enabling seamless transfers and coordinated travel experiences across continents.

      Copa Airlines International Routes

      Copa Airlines maintains a strong route network linking Panama with major cities throughout the Americas. Popular nonstop routes include flights connecting Panama City with destinations such as Miami, New York, Toronto, Mexico City, Bogotá, Lima, Buenos Aires, Santiago, and numerous Caribbean locations. The airline regularly adjusts routes based on passenger demand and seasonal travel trends while strengthening connectivity across the region. Many services are designed to support both business and leisure travelers with convenient connection schedules through its central hub. This hub-based routing structure allows passengers to plan efficient multi-city international itineraries with coordinated baggage handling and synchronized flight connections.
