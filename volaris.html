      About Volaris

      Volaris, founded in 2005 and headquartered in Mexico City, is a leading ultra-low-cost airline serving Mexico, the United States, Central America, and select destinations in South America. The airline focuses on affordable air travel by offering competitive base fares while allowing passengers to customize services according to their needs. Volaris operates major bases in Mexico City, Guadalajara, Tijuana, and Cancún, providing strong cross-border connectivity between Mexico and U.S. cities.       Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Volaris Baggage Policy

      Volaris follows an unbundled fare structure where baggage allowance depends on the fare package selected during booking. Fees vary according to travel route and timing of purchase.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Most fares include one personal item, while carry-on baggage allowance depends on the selected fare bundle. Cabin baggage must comply with airline size and weight restrictions and fit within overhead compartments or beneath the seat.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage is generally not included in basic fares and must be purchased separately unless included in bundled options. Standard checked bags must typically not exceed 158 cm (62 inches) in total dimensions and weight limits usually apply depending on fare conditions. Additional fees may apply for overweight or oversized baggage. Special items such as sports equipment may be transported according to airline policies.
      Passengers may add carry-on or checked baggage while booking Volaris flight tickets through our platform.
