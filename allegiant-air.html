      About Allegiant Air

      Allegiant Air, founded in 1997, is an American ultra-low-cost airline headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada. The airline focuses on providing affordable leisure travel by connecting smaller regional cities with popular vacation destinations across the United States. Allegiant operates a point-to-point network serving more than 120 destinations, with major operations centered around cities such as Las Vegas, Orlando, Phoenix–Mesa, Tampa/St. Petersburg, and Fort Lauderdale. Exploreease allows travelers to conveniently complete Allegiant Air flight reservations online. Passengers can quickly search and book budget-friendly Allegiant Air tickets through our platform. Allegiant primarily operates independent routes and focuses on nonstop leisure travel rather than global alliance partnerships.

      Allegiant Air Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for Allegiant Air flights either at airport counters or through online check-in services. Online check-in is generally recommended because it speeds up the boarding process and helps travelers avoid airport queues, although certain travel cases may require in-person verification. For check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete airport procedures. Airport check-in typically closes about 45 minutes before departure for domestic flights. Passengers are advised to confirm exact timing requirements depending on the departure airport.
      2. Online Check-in: Allegiant Air online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and remains available until shortly before scheduled takeoff. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile platform and download digital boarding passes.

      Allegiant Air Frequent Flyer Program

      Allegiant Air offers the Allways Rewards program, designed to help travelers earn points on flights and vacation purchases. Members can accumulate points through Allegiant flight bookings, hotel packages, car rentals, and purchases made using eligible co-branded credit cards.
      Earned points may be redeemed toward future flight bookings or travel packages.
      The program focuses on simple earning and redemption benefits rather than traditional elite status tiers.

      Baggage Allowance & Booking

      Allegiant Air Baggage Policy

      Baggage allowances with Allegiant Air depend on fare selection and additional services purchased during booking, as the airline follows an ultra-low-cost model where most extras are optional.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are allowed one personal item free of charge that must fit under the seat. Carry-on bags require an additional fee and must not exceed 22 x 16 x 10 inches.
      Personal items should remain within airline size guidelines to avoid additional charges.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage is not included in base fares and must be purchased separately. Standard checked bags generally must not exceed 50 lbs in weight and 80 linear inches in total dimensions.
      Fees may vary depending on route, season, and whether baggage is added during booking or at the airport.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment may be accepted according to airline rules.
      Passengers can add baggage options while booking Allegiant Air flight tickets through our platform.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Allegiant Air Flight Changes

      Allegiant Air allows itinerary changes depending on ticket conditions and optional protection services selected during booking.       International Flights & Routes

      Allegiant Air International Flights

      Allegiant Air primarily operates domestic leisure routes within the United States, with limited international services to nearby vacation destinations such as Mexico and the Caribbean depending on seasonal operations. The airline focuses on nonstop point-to-point travel rather than long-haul global flights. Cabin options generally include standard economy seating with optional upgrades such as extra legroom seats. Onboard services are offered as paid add-ons, allowing passengers to customize their travel experience according to budget preferences. Allegiant’s operational model emphasizes affordable vacation travel supported by simplified onboard services and direct routes to popular tourist destinations.

      Allegiant Air International Routes

      Allegiant Air maintains a leisure-focused route network connecting regional U.S. airports with major vacation markets. Popular routes include nonstop services to destinations such as Las Vegas, Orlando, Tampa Bay, Phoenix-Mesa, and seasonal routes to beach destinations in Mexico. The airline regularly adjusts routes based on seasonal demand and tourism trends, emphasizing nonstop travel from underserved cities. Unlike traditional network carriers, Allegiant focuses on direct routes rather than connecting hubs, enabling travelers to reach vacation destinations quickly and affordably. This strategy supports both leisure travelers and families seeking convenient and budget-friendly travel options across its expanding route network.
