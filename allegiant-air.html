      About Allegiant Air

      Allegiant Air, founded in 1997 and headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada, is an American ultra-low-cost airline focused on connecting smaller regional cities with popular leisure destinations. The airline operates a point-to-point network serving more than 120 destinations across the United States, primarily linking underserved airports with vacation locations such as Florida, Nevada, Arizona, and California. Exploreease allows travelers to make Allegiant Air flight reservations easily through an online booking experience. Passengers can quickly search and secure affordable Allegiant Air tickets while also accessing selected promotional deals on popular routes. Allegiant follows a low-fare model that allows travelers to customize trips by adding only the services they need, helping reduce overall travel costs.

      Services and Amenities

      Allegiant Air Check-in Methods

      Passengers may check in for Allegiant Air flights online or at airport counters. Online check-in is strongly recommended because Allegiant operates primarily from smaller airports and encourages digital boarding passes for faster processing. Certain passengers may still need airport assistance depending on travel requirements. For any help related to check-in or boarding passes, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers should arrive early to complete check-in procedures. Airport check-in typically closes about 45 minutes before scheduled departure, and passengers are advised to verify timing guidelines before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Allegiant online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and usually remains available until shortly before takeoff through the airline’s website or mobile platform.

      Allegiant Air Frequent Flyer Program

      Allegiant Air does not operate a traditional mileage-based frequent flyer program. Instead, travelers can benefit from savings through the Allegiant World Mastercard® and promotional travel offers that provide rewards, priority services, and travel discounts.
      Cardholders may earn points on eligible purchases that can be redeemed toward future travel bookings and vacation packages.
      This simplified rewards structure aligns with Allegiant’s low-cost travel model focused on optional add-on benefits rather than tier-based memberships.

      Baggage Allowance

      Allegiant Air Baggage Policy

      Allegiant Air follows an à-la-carte baggage policy where most services are optional and priced separately depending on traveler preferences.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: A personal item such as a small backpack or handbag is included with every ticket. Standard carry-on bags require an additional fee unless included in a bundled fare package. Carry-on size limits generally must not exceed 22 inches x 16 inches x 10 inches.
      2. Checked Baggage: Checked baggage is not included in base fares and must be purchased separately. Standard checked bags typically follow a maximum linear dimension of 80 inches and a weight limit of 50 lbs.
      Additional fees may apply for overweight or oversized baggage.
      Special items such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline guidelines.
      Passengers may add baggage while booking Allegiant Air flights through our platform.

      Airline Changes & Cancellation

      Airline Changes

      Allegiant Air allows itinerary adjustments based on ticket type, travel dates, and fare conditions selected during booking. Most reservations can be modified online through the manage travel section, though flexibility varies depending on the purchased fare and any added Trip Flex protection. Passengers may change flight dates, times, or destinations subject to availability on alternative flights. Changes made without Trip Flex may include applicable modification fees along with any increase in fare. Travelers who purchased flexible options may update bookings with reduced or waived change fees within permitted timelines. Same-day or last-minute changes depend on seat availability and current fare rules. When updating a reservation, any difference in airfare, taxes, or related charges must be paid. Reviewing ticket conditions beforehand helps passengers adjust travel plans confidently and avoid unexpected issues.

      Airline Cancellation

      Allegiant Air provides cancellation options that vary depending on fare type and purchased travel protection. Tickets canceled within 24 hours of booking may qualify for a full refund when eligible under airline policies. Most standard fares are non-refundable, but travelers may receive future travel credits after applicable fees. Customers who purchase Trip Flex during booking may modify or cancel flights without change fees within specified limits. If Allegiant cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, passengers may request refunds or alternate travel arrangements. Policies may vary depending on promotional fares, so reviewing ticket terms before canceling is recommended.

      International Flights & Routes

      International Flights

      Allegiant Air primarily focuses on domestic leisure travel within the United States but also operates limited international services to nearby vacation destinations such as Mexico and the Caribbean. The airline’s network emphasizes nonstop flights connecting smaller cities directly to popular tourist locations, reducing travel time and avoiding large hub connections. Onboard services are streamlined in line with its ultra-low-cost model, with optional purchases available for snacks, beverages, and added comfort features.

      International Routes

      Allegiant Air maintains a selective international route network focused mainly on leisure markets. Popular routes include nonstop flights from regional U.S. airports to destinations in Mexico and select Caribbean locations. Routes are adjusted seasonally based on travel demand and vacation trends. The airline’s point-to-point structure allows travelers to reach vacation destinations directly without connecting flights, making travel simpler and often more affordable for leisure passengers seeking convenient nonstop options.
