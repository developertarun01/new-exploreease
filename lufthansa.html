      About Lufthansa

      Lufthansa, founded in 1953, is the flag carrier of Germany and one of Europe’s largest full-service airlines, with headquarters located in Cologne. The airline operates an extensive global network connecting destinations across Europe, Asia, the Americas, Africa, and the Middle East. Its primary hubs are Frankfurt Airport and Munich Airport, both serving as major international gateways for long-haul and regional travel. Exploreease allows travelers to make Lufthansa flight reservations easily through a convenient online platform. You can quickly search and book competitive Lufthansa flight tickets while comparing multiple travel options. We also feature selected Lufthansa flight deals on popular routes, helping travelers plan cost-effective journeys. Lufthansa is a founding member of Star Alliance and maintains codeshare partnerships with numerous global airlines, enabling seamless connectivity to destinations worldwide.

      Services and Amenities

      Lufthansa Check-in Methods

      Passengers may complete check-in for Lufthansa flights through airport counters, self-service kiosks, or online and mobile check-in services. Digital check-in options are generally preferred as they offer flexibility and help reduce waiting time at the airport. In certain travel situations, passengers may still need to complete check-in procedures directly at the airport counter. For any check-in or boarding pass assistance, travelers may contact Exploreease customer support.
      1. Airport Check-in: Travelers must arrive at the airport to finalize check-in procedures. Check-in counters usually close around 40 minutes before domestic and European departures and about 60 minutes prior to most intercontinental flights. Passengers should confirm exact timelines before travel.
      2. Online Check-in: Lufthansa online check-in typically opens 23 hours before departure and remains available until shortly before scheduled takeoff depending on the route. Passengers can check in using the airline’s official website or mobile application.

      Lufthansa Frequent Flyer Program

      Miles & More is the airline’s loyalty rewards program designed to provide members with mileage earning and redemption opportunities. Members can earn miles through Lufthansa and Star Alliance partner flights, hotel stays, car rentals, credit cards, shopping partners, and travel services connected with the program.
      Accumulated miles may be redeemed for award flights, cabin upgrades, lounge access, and other travel benefits.
      The program includes multiple membership tiers such as Frequent Traveller, Senator, and HON Circle, offering enhanced privileges including priority services and additional baggage allowance.

      Baggage Allowance

      Lufthansa Baggage Policy

      Lufthansa baggage allowance varies depending on route, fare type, and travel class. Complimentary checked baggage is included on many fares, especially on long-haul international routes and premium cabins.
      1. Carry-On Baggage: Passengers are typically permitted one cabin bag and one personal item at no additional cost. Cabin baggage must meet airline size limits and usually weighs up to 8 kg in Economy and Premium Economy Class, while Business and First Class passengers may carry additional cabin baggage.
      Personal items such as handbags or laptop bags must fit under the seat or within overhead compartments.
      2. Checked Baggage: Complimentary checked baggage allowance depends on fare family and cabin class. Economy Class passengers may receive at least one checked bag on intercontinental routes, while Business and First Class passengers are permitted higher baggage limits.
      Miles & More elite members may receive additional baggage privileges depending on membership level.
      Special baggage such as sports equipment or musical instruments may be transported according to airline regulations.
      Passengers can add or manage baggage while booking Lufthansa flight tickets through our platform.
